## The Author

**Ian Timotheos Pilon** — Product designer, UX researcher, author. Career arc: forklift operator → Toyota dispatch → UX practitioner → Head of Product Design (IOHK) → Author. Co-founder of ETHWaterloo. Creator of NeuroKick. Based in Waterloo, Ontario.

## Mentors and Influences

- **Dr. Deming** — Quality management pioneer; mentored Bob Moesta; influenced Toyota Production System
- **Dr. Taguchi** — Quality engineering; mentored Bob Moesta; influenced Toyota methods
- **Don Norman** — User-centered design principles; referenced by Nick Shackleton-Jones
- **Darrell O'Donnell** — Executive coach who recommended the leadership book on "accidental diminishing behavior" to Ian
- **Rob Miller** — Ian's colleague at Toyota dispatch who helped him overcome fear of computers
- **Ben (Spurry CEO)** — Amjad Hassoun's CEO who introduced him to generative AI
- **Charles Hoskinson** — Founder of Cardano/IOHK; Ian's employer during the blockchain period

## Historical and Contemporary Figures Referenced

- **Steve Wozniak** — Apple co-founder; Scoble's friend and mentor
- **Bill Gates** — Microsoft founder; Scoble interviewed him
- **Vic Gundotra** — Microsoft executive who hired Scoble; later funded Android at Google
- **Elon Musk** — Gave Scoble the first Tesla ride
- **Adam Cheyer** — Siri co-founder; cited by Scoble on ChatGPT's surprising impact
- **Carl Sagan** — Inspired Mark Rubin's global eco-project vision
- **Buckminster Fuller** — Co-inspiration (with Sagan) for Rubin's Long Game Framework
- **Claude Shannon** — Information theory pioneer; used as visual metaphor for context resolution
- **F. Scott Fitzgerald** — Quoted by Jonas on holding opposing ideas simultaneously
- **Clotaire Rapaille** — Brand strategist; "The Culture Code" referenced by Ian
- **Derek Cabrera & Laura Colosi** — DSRP thinking framework ("Thinking at Every Desk")
- **Carlos E. Perez** — Twitter post that connected Ian with Alexander Naumenko
- **Lex Fridman** — Podcast where Ian discovered Michael Levin
- **Ryan Singer** — Co-author of "Shape Up" with Bob Moesta
- **Paul LeBlanc** — President of Southern New Hampshire University (SNHU case study)
- **Vitalik Buterin** — Ethereum founder; was classmates with Liam Horn in Waterloo
- **Liam Horn** — Led Atomic Labs; co-founded ETHWaterloo with Ian
- **Hermann Ebbinghaus** — Identified the forgetting curve
- **Michael Sandel** — Trolley problem; referenced by Nick Shackleton-Jones
- **Dave Winer** — RSS creator; early podcasting; Scoble worked for him
- **John Draper "Captain Crunch"** — Hacker legend; attended Woz's Super Bowl party with Scoble
- **Doug Engelbart** — Mother of all demos; worked at SRI where Scoble visited
