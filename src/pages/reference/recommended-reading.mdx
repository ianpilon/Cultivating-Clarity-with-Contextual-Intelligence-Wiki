## Books

| Title | Author | Referenced By | Context |
|-------|--------|--------------|---------|
| **How People Learn** | Nick Shackleton-Jones | Ian Pilon | Source of the Affective Context Model |
| **Demand-Side Sales 101** | Bob Moesta | Bob Moesta | Reframing sales as helping customers make progress |
| **Learning to Build** | Bob Moesta | Bob Moesta | Codifying the learning process for new products |
| **Shape Up** | Ryan Singer & Bob Moesta (Basecamp) | Bob Moesta | Alternative to scrum/agile; available free from Basecamp |
| **The Culture Code** | Clotaire Rapaille | Ian Pilon | Brand strategy and subconscious consumer motivations |
| **Thinking at Every Desk** | Derek Cabrera & Laura Colosi | Ian Pilon | DSRP thinking framework |
| **Age of Context** | Robert Scoble (& Shel Israel) | Robert Scoble | How context drives the future of computing |
| **Context Changes Everything** | (referenced by Naumenko) | Alexander Naumenko | Deep exploration of context; discovered via Twitter |
| Leadership book on "accidental diminishing behavior" | (unnamed, rec'd by Darrell O'Donnell) | Ian Pilon | Sparked the NeuroKick concept |

- **DSRP (Distinctions, Systems, Relationships, Perspectives)** — Derek Cabrera
- **Toyota Production System** — Kaizen, continuous improvement
- **Information Foraging Theory** — Peter Pirolli

- **Peak-End Rule** — Daniel Kahneman
- **Shannon Information Theory** — Claude Shannon (used as visual metaphor)
