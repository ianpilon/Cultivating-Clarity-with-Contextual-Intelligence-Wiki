## The Central Thesis

True innovation in software development stems not from the speed of creation but from the depth of using contextual intelligence to gather, inform, and understand customers' physical and psychological **struggling moments** that are preventing them from making progress toward their goals.

Value is not software fulfilling a set of business and technical requirements, but how the tool resolves micro-interactions of struggling moments of the human experience that are in the way of the customer's ability to move forward on their journey of striving to become a better version of themselves.
